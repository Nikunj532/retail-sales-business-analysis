# Retail Sales Business Analysis

## Overview
This project analyzes retail transaction data to identify opportunities to improve profit margins while maintaining revenue.

The analysis evaluates customer behavior, store performance, marketing campaign impact, and product strategy.

## Business Questions
1. Identify the top customers purchasing across multiple stores.
2. Evaluate the revenue impact of the TV marketing campaign.
3. Identify a store that could be closed with minimal impact.
4. Evaluate the impact of removing Product C.

## Dataset
The dataset contains retail transaction records including:

- Customer_ID
- Transaction_date
- Order_ID
- Revenue
- Margin
- Product_ID
- Units
- Store_ID

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Seaborn
- Matplotlib
- Jupyter Notebook

## Key Insights
- Multi-store customers were identified as ideal targets for marketing campaigns.
- The TV campaign increased revenue during September 2012.
- Store LA_333 is the weakest performing store and could be considered for closure.
- Removing Product C reduces both revenue and profit.
